# NXCLI Branding & Naming Research

This document summarizes the strategic naming and branding research conducted for **NXCLI** (formerly Mesh Agent). It serves as a foundation for future commercialization and SaaS-level expansion.

## 1. Current Identity: NXCLI (v2.4)
- **Primary Name:** NXCLI (Nexaline CLI)
- **Pronunciation:** "Nex-el-CLI"
- **Mission:** The Multimodal Agent Orchestrator.
- **Core Loop:** Context-aware task decomposition and serial piping between specialized agents.

## 2. Competitive Context (Research Findings)
- **Nexus/Mesh:** High saturation (Nexus IT, Mesh Wi-Fi, Mesh App).
- **VibeNexus:** Taken by a music/entertainment platform.
- **Sonata:** Heavily taken by Sonata Software Ltd (Nasdaq: NXL) and the PHP Sonata Project.
- **Baton:** Used by ConductorOne (Security) and American Express (Load Testing).
- **Orchestrata:** Used by a creative agency and a VST music library.

## 3. Commercial Alternatives (Future Proofing)
If NXCLI ever moves to a dedicated SaaS product, these are the top "Clean" and "Available" candidates:

### Direction A: The "Orchestra" (Leadership & Harmony)
- **MaestroAI:** Authoritative, premium, and intelligent.
- **Orchra:** (Short for Orchestra). 6 letters, fast to type, unique.
- **OrchX:** (Orchestra X). Ultra-modern, 4 letters, sounds like a next-gen tool.
- **BatonLink:** A pivot on "Baton" that avoids the CLI naming conflict.

### Direction B: The "Connective" (Structure & Flow)
- **BasedLayer:** (Lingo: "Based"). A foundational layer for your agent stack.
- **LatticeNode:** Sophisticated, implies a repeating pattern of intelligence.
- **Synap.ai:** (Short for Synapse). Focuses on the "gap" where context jumps between agents.
- **PlexusCLI:** Technical, medical-grade network terminology.

### Direction C: The "Shipping" (Productivity & Speed)
- **ShipMesh:** Focuses on the "Just ship it" developer mindset.
- **CookNode:** (Lingo: "Let him cook"). "Let your agents cook."
- **AgentThread:** (Lingo: Twitter "Threads"). Plays on the serial task passing.

## 4. Visual Identity Concepts
- **Palette:** Cyan (#00FFFF) and Magenta (#FF00FF). Modern, high-contrast, "Cyber-SaaS" aesthetic.
- **Logo Theme:** A conductor’s baton interacting with a neural node, or an interlocking "N" and "X" representing the nexus.
